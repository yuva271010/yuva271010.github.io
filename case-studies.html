Case Studies

Short, scannable stories (Problem → Approach → Impact → Tech).

1) Enterprise Analytics Platform (Microsoft Fabric + Power BI)

  • Problem: Fragmented reporting, inconsistent KPIs, and slow decision cycles.
  • Approach: Designed an end-to-end platform: data marts + semantic layer, governance workflows, workspace standards, and RLS patterns.
  • Impact: Enabled 400+ users across 10 departments; reduced decision latency from weeks to hours.
  • Tech: Microsoft Fabric (OneLake, Lakehouse/Warehouse, Dataflows Gen2, Pipelines, Notebooks/PySpark) + Power BI (semantic models, DAX, RLS).

2) Salesforce + ERP Harmonization (Commercial Performance)

  • Problem: Misaligned CRM/ERP data affecting incentives, reconciliation, and forecasting confidence.
  • Approach: Migrated and harmonized CRM data, aligned master data with ERP, and standardized KPI definitions used by Sales and Finance.
  • Impact: Migrated 3M+ Salesforce records; improved incentive/SPIFF tracking and revenue reconciliation accuracy across regions.
  • Tech: Salesforce Sales Cloud, MuleSoft/Scribe integration, SQL, Power BI.

3) Forecasting Enablement (Analytics + ML)

  • Problem: Limited forward-looking visibility for bookings and inventory planning.
  • Approach: Python-based EDA and feature refinement, then applied ML techniques to generate forecasting insights and expose them via Power BI.
  • Impact: Improved forecast usability by delivering interactive insights for commercial planning and operational decisioning.
  • Tech: Python, notebooks, decision trees / regression models, Power BI dashboards.
