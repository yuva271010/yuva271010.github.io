1

Enterprise Analytics Platform (Datawarehousing + Power BI)

Problem

Legacy reporting landscape produced siloed datasets, conflicting KPI definitions, and slow cross‑functional insights across Finance, Sales, Operations, and Supply Chain. Business spent most of the time is building report rather than acting on it.

Approach

Designed and implemented an enterprise‑wide analytics platform using Microsoft Fabric. Built a OneLake Lakehouse with a medallion architecture (Bronze/Silver/Gold), standardized semantic models, workspace governance, and RBAC/RLS aligned to organizational hierarchy. Automated deployment and model lifecycle using Azure DevOps CI/CD pipelines.

Impact

400+ platform users across 10 departments; reporting latency reduced from weeks to hours; self-service analytics adoption enabled enterprise-wide.

Microsoft Fabric Power BI WhereScape Lakehouse Dataflows Gen2 Kimball / Star Schema Agile
2

Data Migration (CRM and ERP)

Problem

Rapid business growth and evolving CRM/ERP platforms created mismatches between legacy data and new system structures during migration, putting day‑one operational continuity at risk if critical customer, financial, and transactional data wasn’t transformed,migrated cleanly

Approach

Defined a structured migration framework covering data assessment, cleansing, transformation, validation, and multiple mock cutovers, supported by automated pipelines and business‑aligned mapping rules to ensure accuracy and readiness. Spontaneous reconcilation results in BI tools and migration data handles in BI tools innovatively.

Impact

3M+ Salesforce records migrated; ERP Migration done with 98% accuracy;Delivered a seamless day one go‑live with complete, validated data; ensured uninterrupted business continuity; and established a clean, reliable data foundation for future scalability.

Salesforce Sales Cloud Power BI Scribe SQL Data Migration Mulesoft Microsoft AX Data Ware House
3

Forecasting Enablement (Analytics + ML)

Problem

Minimal forward-looking visibility for bookings, Revenue and inventory planning teams relied on static manual spreadsheet models with long turnaround cycles, till finance monthly closure.

Approach

All transactional data insights were available to business with a latency of just 4 hrs in Power BI; generated revenue forecasting based on historical opportunity data, wins loss ratio, and backlog data in Power BI; Python-based EDA and feature engineering- to identify unknown parameters influencing the sales seasonality .

Impact

Interactive forecast dashboards replaced spreadsheet models; commercial planning and operational decisioning improved; Visibility of the revenue and inventory planning improved significantly.

Power BI Python PySpark Jupyter Notebooks Linear Regression
4

Sales Commissioning Software

Problem

For Monthly sales commmissioning process, the sales/ Compensation and Finance team was using excel based manual calculation and it was taking around 2 weeks to calculate the commission for each sales rep. The process was human error prone and time consuming.

Approach

Learnt, developed and Deployed sales commissioning software (SPIFF) to automate the calculation process, integrating data from multiple sources for bookings,revenue and applying business rules to ensure accuracy and efficiency.

Impact

Reduced the commissioning calculation time from 2 weeks to just a few hours, sales rep and managers were able to view thier performance on day by day perspective. The automation of the process also reduced the risk of human error, ensuring that sales representatives were compensated accurately and on time.

Salesforce.com SPIFF SQL Database Mulesoft
