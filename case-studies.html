1

Enterprise Analytics Platform (Microsoft Fabric + Power BI)

Problem

Fragmented reporting, inconsistent KPIs, and slow decision cycles across 10 business departments Finance, Sales, Operations, Supply Chain, and more.

Approach

Designed an end-to-end platform: OneLake Lakehouse with medallion architecture (Bronze/Silver/Gold), governed semantic models, workspace governance, RBAC/RLS aligned to org structure, and CI/CD pipelines via Azure DevOps.

Impact

400+ platform users across 10 departments; reporting latency reduced from weeks to hours; self-service analytics adoption enabled enterprise-wide.

Microsoft Fabric Power BI DAX RLS OneLake Lakehouse Dataflows Gen2 PySpark Notebooks Kimball / Star Schema Azure DevOps CI/CD
2

Salesforce + ERP Harmonisation (Commercial Performance)

Problem

Misaligned CRM and ERP data causing errors in incentive calculations, revenue reconciliation, and forecasting confidence across Sales and Finance.

Approach

Migrated and harmonised CRM data (3M+ records), aligned master data with ERP for SPIFF tracking, standardised KPI definitions shared across Sales and Finance using MuleSoft and Scribe for integration.

Impact

3M+ Salesforce records migrated at 98% accuracy; improved SPIFF/incentive tracking and revenue reconciliation; E-Commerce product store implemented end-to-end.

Salesforce Sales Cloud Power BI MuleSoft Scribe SQL Data Migration Master Data Management Microsoft AX / D365 F&O
3

Forecasting Enablement (Analytics + ML)

Problem

Minimal forward-looking visibility for bookings and inventory planning teams relied on static manual spreadsheet models with long turnaround cycles.

Approach

Impact

Interactive forecast dashboards replaced spreadsheet models; commercial planning and operational decisioning improved; Copilot AI-augmented workflows enabled for ongoing iteration.
